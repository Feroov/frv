Welcome to the city of two continents!

Istanbul, located in the Marmara Region of Turkey is the largest city in all of 81 provinces of Turkey. Istanbul is a crucial city in regards of economic, historical and socio-cultural aspects. It is ranked as the 34th in the world in terms of economy. It is also ranked the 1st in Europe and 6th in the world by population. The population of Istanbul is 15.8 million as of 2022. Istanbul was also known as Constantinople from the Byzantine times.
